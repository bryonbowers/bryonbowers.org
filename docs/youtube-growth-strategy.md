## Strategy 1: Content Bridge Videos

### Music Visualizers / Lyric Videos
- Create visually engaging videos for each song on your main YouTube channel
- Include Spotify/YouTube Music links in description AND as pinned comment
- Add end screens with "Listen on Spotify" call-to-action
- Use YouTube Shorts (60 sec clips) to tease full songs

### Behind-the-Scenes Content
- "Making of [Song Name]" videos showing your creative process
- Studio sessions, songwriting breakdowns
- Always end with: "Full song available on Spotify and YouTube Music"

### Story Time + Music
- Tell the story behind each song in a dedicated video
- Play the song at the end
- This creates emotional connection AND drives streams

## Strategy 3: Cross-Promotion Tactics

### YouTube Shorts Strategy
- Post 15-60 second clips of your best song moments
- Use trending sounds/formats when relevant
- Always include text overlay: "Full song on Spotify"
- Post 3-5 Shorts per week for maximum reach

### Playlist Strategy on YouTube Music
- Create themed playlists featuring your songs + similar artists
- "Songs for Late Night Drives" (include 3-4 of your tracks)
- "The Dead Fish Poet Collection" (all your music)
- Share these playlists on your main channel

### Collaboration Content
- React to other artists' music (builds community)
- Collaborate with other musicians for cross-promotion
- Feature on each other's YouTube channels

## Strategy 5: Release Campaign Template

### Week Before Release
- Day 7: Teaser clip (15 sec) on Shorts
- Day 5: Behind-the-scenes photo/story
- Day 3: 30-second preview clip
- Day 1: "Tomorrow" announcement post

### Release Day
- Full song video on YouTube
- Community post with all streaming links
- Short clip with "OUT NOW" text overlay
- Pin comment with links

### Week After Release
- Thank you video/post
- Share listener reactions
- Create a "listening party" livestream
- Post lyric breakdown Short

## Strategy 6: Spotify-Specific Growth

### Pre-Save Campaigns
- Use services like DistroKid's HyperFollow or Feature.fm
- Create a landing page for each release
- Promote pre-saves on YouTube 2 weeks before release

### Spotify Canvas
- Create 3-8 second looping videos for each track
- These play when someone listens on mobile
- Use visuals from your YouTube content

### Spotify for Artists
- Claim your profile if you haven't
- Add artist bio, photos, and social links
- Submit songs to editorial playlists 4 weeks before release
- Track which YouTube content drives the most streams

---

## Strategy 7: YouTube Music Optimization

### Channel Setup
- Ensure official artist channel is verified
- Link to your main YouTube channel
- Add complete bio and social links

### Content for YouTube Music
- Upload official audio videos (static image + song)
- These show up in YouTube Music searches
- Use consistent branding/artwork

### YouTube Music Playlists
- Create and share playlists regularly
- Include your songs alongside popular artists
- Share playlist links on your main channel
